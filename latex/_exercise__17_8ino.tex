\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Demonstration of connecting to Wi\+Fi and sending data to Thing\+Speak. 

This sketch connects the ESP32 to the specified Wi\+Fi, then periodically sends two fields of data (RSSI and a digital pin reading) to a Thing\+Speak channel. It also demonstrates reading back from the channel.
